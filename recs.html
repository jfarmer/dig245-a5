Recommendations!

For a better user experience, Instagram should include:

  1. The ability to sort through one's own posts by number of likes and number of comments
  2. The ability to sort one's own posts to be displayed in chronological order, instead of only reverse chronological
  3. Provide the actual number of followers shared with another user

Some Highlights from the Test:

  1. Having the suggested followers list in multiple places on the website
  2. Sorting comments on posts by most popular (at least on posts that receive a ton of traffic, like Beyonce's)
